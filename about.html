About 18 Karat Nails

Our Story

Founded in 2024, 18 Karat Nails was born from a passion for artistry and a commitment to excellence. Located in the heart of Nairobi at Lavington Square Mall, we bring luxury nail services to those who appreciate quality and precision.

Our name, 18 Karat, represents our standard of service - pure, premium, and built to last. Every client who walks through our doors experiences the gold standard of nail care.

Meet Your Nail Tech

Cherono is a certified nail technician with over 5 years of experience in the beauty industry. Specializing in BIAB, Gel X extensions, and custom nail art, Cherono has trained with leading professionals across Kenya and East Africa.

"Every nail is a canvas, and I treat each one like a masterpiece," says Cherono. "My goal is to make every client feel luxurious and confident."

Our Values

✅ Quality: We use only premium products from trusted brands.
✅ Hygiene: All tools are sterilized after every use.
✅ Artistry: Every design is customized to your style.
✅ Care: Your natural nails are our priority - we never damage them.
