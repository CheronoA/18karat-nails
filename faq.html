💎 Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your appointment

💅 How long do BIAB or Gel X extensions last? ▼

With proper care, BIAB and Gel X extensions typically last 3-4 weeks. We recommend booking a fill appointment every 3 weeks to maintain the look and strength of your nails.

💸 Is there a deposit required to book? ▼

Yes, we require a KES 500 non-refundable deposit to secure your appointment. This amount goes toward your final bill.

⏰ What happens if I'm late? ▼

We kindly ask you to arrive on time. If you're more than 15 minutes late, we may need to shorten your service or reschedule. A KES 200 late fee may apply.

🧼 How do I care for my nails after the appointment? ▼

We recommend applying cuticle oil daily, wearing gloves for household chores, and avoiding using your nails as tools. Your nail tech will give you detailed aftercare instructions.

🎨 Can I bring my own design ideas? ▼

Absolutely! We love when clients bring inspiration photos. You can DM us on Instagram @18karatnails or bring a picture to your appointment.

🚫 What if I need to cancel? ▼

Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance will incur a KES 500 fee. Deposits are non-refundable in all cases.
